I am an artist and designer based on the unceded Coast Salish Territory of the Lekwungen and W̱SÁNEĆ nations ("Victoria, BC, Canada"). My work spans animation, sound, game design, digital media, and painting. A few of the themes I explore are identity, grief, emotional processing and alternative ways of structuring society. I’m drawn to process-driven approaches, and enjoy layering forms and textures to create a sense of balance within fragmented or unsettled spaces.
