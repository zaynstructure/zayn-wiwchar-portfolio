Layered Fragments

I am an artist and designer based on the unceded Coast Salish Territory of the Lekwungen and W̠SÁNEČ nations ("Victoria, BC, Canada"). My work spans animation, sound, game design, digital media, and painting. I like to explore themes of identity, grief, emotional processing, and alternative ways of structuring society in an abstracted way.

The conventions I use visually also shape my approach to music and sound, where I experiment with texture, layering, and form to create immersive, expressive environments. Collaboration and experimentation are central to my practice, always underpinned by a decolonial and decentralizing perspective that draws from my background in social work. At its core, my work is about using art as a tool for resistance, healing, and reimagining new possibilities.
