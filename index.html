I am an artist and designer based on the unceded Coast Salish Territory of the Lekwungen and W̱SÁNEĆ nations ("Victoria, BC, Canada"). My work spans animation, sound, game design, digital media, and painting, exploring themes of identity, grief, emotional processing, and alternative ways of structuring society.


This same sensibility shapes my approach to music and sound, where I experiment with texture, layering, and form to create immersive, expressive environments. Collaboration and experimentation are central to my practice, informed by a decolonial and decentralizing perspective.
